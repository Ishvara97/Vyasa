[Hymn 1.1]

[Verse 1]

DEV: अग्निमीळे पुरोहितं यज्ञस्य देवं रत्वीजम | होतारं रत्नधातमम ||
IAST:agnim īḻe purohitaṃ yajñasya devaṃ ratvījama | hotāraṃ ratnadhātamama ||
ENG: I Laud Agni, the chosen Priest, God, minister of sacrifice, The hotar, lavishest of wealth.

[Verse 2]

DEV: अग्निः पूर्वेभिर्र्षिभिरीड्यो नूतनैरुत | स देवानेह वक्षति ||
IAST: aghniḥ pūrvebhirṛṣibhirīḍyo nūtanairuta | sa devāneha vakṣati ||
ENG: Worthy is Agni to be praised by living as by ancient seers. He shall bring hitherward the Gods.

[Verse 3]

DEV: अग्निना रयिमश्नवत्पोषमेव दिवेदिवे । यशसं वीरवत्तमम् ॥
IAST: agninā rayim aśnavat poṣam eva divedive | yaśasaṃ vīravattamam ||
ENG: Through Agni man obtaineth wealth, yea, plenty waxing day by day, most rich in heroes, glorious.

[Verse 4]

DEV: अग्ने यं यज्ञमध्वरं विश्वतः परिभूरसि । स इद्देवेषु गच्छति ॥
IAST: agne yaṃ yajñam adhvaraṃ viśvataḥ paribhūr asi | sa id deveṣu gacchati ||
ENG: Agni, the perfect sacrifice which thou encompassest about verily goeth to the Gods.

[Verse 5]

DEV: अग्निर्होता कविक्रतुः सत्यश्चित्रश्रवस्तमः देवो देवेभिरा गमत् ॥
IAST: agnir hotā kavikratuḥ satyaś citraśravastamaḥ devo devebhir ā gamat ||
ENG: May Agni, sapient-minded Priest, truthful, most gloriously great, the God, come hither with the Gods.

[Verse 6]

DEV: यदङ्ग दाशुषे त्वमग्ने भद्रं करिष्यसि तवेतत्सत्यमङ्गिरः ॥
IAST: yad aṅga dāśuṣe tvam agne bhadraṃ kariṣyasi tav etat satyam aṅgiraḥ ||
ENG: Whatever blessing, Agni, thou wilt grant unto thy worshipper, that, Angiras, is indeed thy truth.

[Verse 7]

DEV: उप त्वाग्ने दिवेदिवे दोषावस्तर्धिया वयं नमो भरन्त एमसि ॥
IAST: upa tvāgne divedive doṣāvastar dhiyā vayaṃ namo bharanta emasí ||
ENG: To thee, dispeller of the night, O Agni, day by day with prayer bringing thee reverence, we come.

[Verse 8]

DEV: राजन्तमध्वराणां गोपामृतस्य दीदिविम् वर्धमानं स्वे दमे ॥
IAST: rājantam adhvarāṇāṃ gopām ṛtasya dīdivim vardhamānaṃ sve dame ||
ENG: Ruler of sacrifices, guard of Law eternal, radiant One, increasing in thine own abode.

[Verse 9]

DEV: स नः पितेव सूनवेऽग्ने सुपायनो भव सचस्वा नः स्वस्तये ॥
IAST: sa naḥ piteva sūnave agne supāyano bhava sacasvā naḥ svastaye ||
ENG: Be to us easy of approach, even as a father to his son: Agni, be with us for our weal.
